Instructions
- Click and drag the body of a shape to move it around.
- Drag handles to resize.
- Double click to spawn a new box.
- Double click while holding SHIFT to spawn a new arrow.
- Or...just click the appropriate buttons to add objects.
- Select an object and press the delete key to delete it.
- Shapes are attached to the slide, so flip slides freely.
